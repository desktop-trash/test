ABOUT US

Since 1958 Bernie Electric has been supplying electrical products and services to the Kansas City area. We continue to grow with the help of our dedicated customers. This third generation locally owned family business is committed to providing solutions to the needs of the industry as a full line, full service electrical distributor just as our founder did many years ago. The personal service of our sales staff is tailored to the needs of our commercial, industrial, maintenance, municipal, and electrical contractor customers.
